# Community Module

## Purpose
Create community interaction and support between users.

## Features
- Community posts
- Experiences
- Recommendations
- Local alerts
- Discussions

## Business Rules
- Offensive content must be moderated
- Users can report posts

## Endpoints
GET /community/posts
POST /community/posts
POST /community/report

## Frontend Requirements
- Community feed
- Human and friendly experience
- Emotional support feeling
